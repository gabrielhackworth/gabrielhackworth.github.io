English II Pre-AP Final: Aitch

Our final for English sophomore year was to write a short story that took place in the setting of whichever foreign book we chose to read. I read Moth Smoke by Mohsin Hamid, so mine is set in Pakistan. I decided to write about two children in the midst of some pretty intense family drama.
